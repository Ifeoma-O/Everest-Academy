  • Contribute to society

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a firm foundation in the educational life of children, setting them on a great headstart for life. We strive to educate the whole child while maintaining high standards with respect for all learners. We seek to have our parents and community actively involved in our students' learning.

Our Vision

Our vision is to have an elementary school that will provide the necessary education for children to develop their talents, strengths and abilities. Through high expectations, rigorous teaching and learning in a safe, collaborative environment, all students will become lifelong learners as they participate in a global society.
